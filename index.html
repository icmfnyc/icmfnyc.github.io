Intercollegiate Chamber

Music Festival

March 20-21, 2021

Bringing student musicians together since 2017.

We are excited to announce the fifth annual Intercollegiate Chamber Music Festival! This year's virtual festival will take place on Saturday, March 20 - Sunday, March 21.

Talented musicians from universities across the country will gather virtually for a weekend of workshops with world-renowned professionals, and the opportunity to make music with inspiring peers across institutions. Through performance and conversation, we strive to establish an environment where college musicians can bridge their musical and academic passions in a non-conservatory setting. We are accepting both solo and chamber performances, both live and pre-recorded.

Events

MASTERCLASSES

ICMF 2021 participants will attend virtual masterclasses to study with world-renowned musicians. The masterclass artists will coach our performers, focusing on musicianship and expression, at the same level as one would find in any conservatory master class.

The ICMF 2021 Masterclasses will take place on Saturday, March 20, 2021.

EXPLORING MUSIC IN QUARANTINE: PANEL OF COLUMBIA PROFESSIONALS

Our panel of speakers will provide the audience with the opportunity to hear from leaders at the forefront of modernizing classical music.

We hope to explore the impact of the pandemic on the music industry, and to consider how the musical landscape might be different in coming years.

PERFORMANCE SHOWCASE

ICMF 2021 participants have the opportunity to showcase their accomplishments in a culminating virtual concert.

The ICMF 2021 performance showcase will take place on Sunday, March 21, 2021.

Apply to Perform at ICMF 2021
  • Applications to perform in ICMF 2021 are now open! The deadline to apply is March 13, 2021.
  • There is no audition process and participation is completely free of charge. As an ICMF performer, you will be invited to attend our Masterclasses, our panel of Columbia professionals, the Sightreading Party, and the Performance Showcase. We are accepting both live and pre-recorded performances for the Performance Showcase.
  • Please contact us at cuclassical@gmail.com if you have any questions.
